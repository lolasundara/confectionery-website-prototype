Our Chocolate

The answer to all of your problems. Tired? Eat our chocolate. Not feeling well? Eat our chocolate. Bored? Eat our chocolate.

Here at Coco's our chocolate is handmade each and every day by our specialty chocolate makers, using a variety of different combinations from premium quality ingredients.

We developed our recipes after years of work and research to improve our most extraordinary flavours, just for you.

Our Story

Our journey begins thousands of years ago in the rainforests of Central America, where ancient tribes discovered the power of the mighty cocoa bean.

It continues through centuries, when it was embraced by Coco's great Choco family, who opened their own cocoa houses and chocolate shop.
